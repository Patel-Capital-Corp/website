Our History

Patel Capital Corp has a rich history of innovation and excellence in the financial services industry. Founded in 2000, our journey began with a vision to provide tailored financial solutions that meet the unique needs of our clients. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, expanding our expertise in structured finance, debt capital markets, and equity capital markets.

In 2005, we successfully completed our first major debt capital raise, helping a leading tech company secure the necessary funding to expand its operations. This milestone marked the beginning of our reputation as a trusted partner in the financial industry.

In 2010, we expanded our services to include equity capital markets, providing innovative solutions to help businesses raise capital and achieve their strategic goals. Our team of experienced professionals has continued to grow, bringing a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every project.

Today, Patel Capital Corp is recognized as a leader in the financial services industry, known for our commitment to transparency, open communication, and innovative solutions. We are dedicated to helping our clients achieve financial success and build a strong foundation for future growth.
