Our Services

Comprehensive financial solutions

Structured Finance Solutions

At Patel Capital Corp, our dedicated structured finance team stands at the forefront of financial innovation, offering bespoke financial instruments and strategies designed to meet the unique needs of our clients. Leveraging deep expertise in debt capital markets and equity capital markets, our team is adept at crafting solutions that not only manage risk but also optimize returns.

Securitization

One of the cornerstone offerings of Patel Capital Corp is securitization. This process involves pooling various financial assets and transforming them into marketable securities, thereby providing liquidity and diversifying risk. For instance, by securitizing loans or receivables, we enable businesses to unlock essential working capital, aiding in business funding and growth.

Asset-Backed Financing

Another innovative solution we offer is asset-backed financing. This structured finance option allows companies to secure loans using their tangible or intangible assets as collateral. Whether it is leveraging real estate, receivables, or even intellectual property, asset-backed financing provides a vital business line of credit.

Hybrid Financial Products

Additionally, Patel Capital Corp specializes in developing hybrid financial products, which combine elements of debt and equity to create flexible funding structures. These products are particularly useful in situations requiring a balance between minimizing cost of capital and maintaining capital structure flexibility.

Leveraged Loans

In the realm of leveraged loans, our expertise ensures that clients can access substantial amounts of capital while maintaining manageable levels of debt. By structuring these loans appropriately, businesses can pursue aggressive growth strategies without compromising their financial stability.

Syndicated Debt

Our syndicated debt arrangements further illustrate Patel Capital Corp's capacity to orchestrate large-scale debt capital raises by collaborating with a consortium of lenders. This not only spreads risk but also enhances the borrowing capacity of our clients, providing them with the necessary financial muscle to execute large-scale projects.
