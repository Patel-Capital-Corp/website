Our Services

At Patel Capital Corp, we offer a comprehensive range of financial services designed to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our expertise spans structured finance, debt capital markets, and equity capital markets, ensuring that we can help businesses navigate complex financial landscapes and achieve their strategic goals.

Structured Finance

Our structured finance solutions are tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients. We provide innovative and flexible financing options that help businesses achieve their financial objectives. Our team of experts works closely with clients to understand their unique requirements and develop customized solutions that optimize their capital structure.

Debt Capital Markets

We specialize in debt capital markets, providing clients with access to a wide range of debt instruments. Our expertise in syndicated debt packages, leveraged loans, and business loans ensures that our clients receive the best possible financing options. We work with clients to structure debt capital raises that meet their specific needs and optimize their financial strategy.

Equity Capital Markets

Our equity capital markets team provides innovative solutions to help businesses raise capital and achieve their strategic goals. We offer a range of equity financing options, including private equity and venture capital, to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our team of experienced professionals works closely with clients to develop tailored equity capital strategies that maximize their financial potential.

Mergers and Acquisitions

We provide comprehensive mergers and acquisitions (M&A) services to help businesses achieve their growth objectives. Our team of experts works with clients to identify potential acquisition targets, conduct due diligence, and negotiate terms. We provide strategic advice and support throughout the M&A process, ensuring that our clients achieve their desired outcomes.

Working Capital Solutions

Our working capital solutions are designed to help businesses manage their day-to-day financial needs. We provide a range of financing options, including business lines of credit and working capital loans, to ensure that our clients have the liquidity they need to operate effectively. Our team of experts works with clients to develop customized working capital strategies that optimize their financial performance.
